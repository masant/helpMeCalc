Complex Converter

Some Tips

  1. Choose your converter type.

    2. If you want to convert a complex number Z from rectangular to polar, you should choose rect2pol. Otherwise, if you want to convert from polar to rectangular, you should choose pol2rect

  2. Complete the inputs

    3. Each input must be completed. If you choose rect2pol, you must complete with the Real part and Imaginary part from the complex number. Otherwise, if you use pol2rect, you have to complete with R (modulus) and phi (phase) from the complex number.

    When you choose 'rect2pol', the input 'angle' is used to specify the angle unit of the output 'phi'.

    When you choose 'pol2rect', the input 'angle' specify the angle unit of the input 'phi'

  3. Press the "Run" button

    4. After you complete all the inputs, you just need to press the 'run' buttom and that's all! Easy, right? :)
